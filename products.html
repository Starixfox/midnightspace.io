Platform overview

The Midnight Space Platform

An honest description of what exists today and what we are building. The platform is under active development with working prototypes available now.

Prototype available First product: ~1–2 months First pilot customer: ~2 months
What exists now

Current modules

These are real, working prototypes — not mockups. They are manually curated and functional, though not yet polished into a commercial product.

Prototype

Funding & program database

A manually curated database of space-related funding calls, grants, accelerators, and public programs. Focused initially on Saudi Arabia and the GCC region, with selected global entries.

What it includes
  • → Program name, source, region, sector
  • → Open / closed / recurring status
  • → Estimated funding amounts where public
  • → Application window and deadlines
  • → Links to original sources
Prototype

Basic dashboards

Charts and summary views built from the program database. Designed for leadership-level overviews rather than deep data exploration.

What it shows
  • → Distribution of programs by region and sector
  • → Open vs. closed vs. upcoming count
  • → Funding volume breakdown where available
  • → Filterable list view with sorting
What's coming

Planned modules

These features are on the roadmap and subject to change based on pilot partner feedback. They do not exist yet.

Planned

Global space funding view

Expand coverage beyond GCC to include major space funding programs from Europe, North America, Asia, and emerging markets. One place for global program intelligence.

Planned

API access

Structured data access for organisations that want to integrate Midnight Space data into their own internal tools, reports, or workflows via a simple API.

Planned

Custom views for agencies

Tailored dashboard configurations for specific agency mandates — filtered to relevant sectors, regions, and program types defined by each organisation's remit.

Planned

Signals & alerts

Automated notifications when new programs open, deadlines approach, or data changes. Delivered as email digests or in-platform alerts depending on preference.

Planned

Exports & reports

Export filtered datasets, generate summary reports, and share curated views with colleagues or stakeholders — in formats suited for briefings and board papers.

Planned

Ecosystem intelligence

Deeper analytics — trend views, scenario comparisons, gap analysis between programs. For organisations doing strategic planning at a sector or national level.

How it will be delivered

Web-based, access by invitation

The Midnight Space platform is delivered as a web-based dashboard — accessible via browser with no software to install. There is no public sign-up. Initial access is by invitation only, through an early access waitlist.

Early pilot partners will receive direct access and have direct input into the product roadmap. The first paid access tier will follow once the platform reaches a usable state — targeted within approximately two months.
