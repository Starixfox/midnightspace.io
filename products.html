Products

We are building space data tools that turn fragmented government and ecosystem information into something readable, navigable, and useful.

PRODUCT 01

Saudi Space Opportunity Hub

The first working prototype from Midnight Space. A manually curated, filterable dashboard that consolidates space funding calls, government programs, accelerators, and grants across the GCC and broader MENA region.

Designed for teams who need to stay on top of what programs are open, closing, or planned — without spending days pulling information from fragmented government sources.

  • Manually curated database of space funding and programs
  • Filters by region, sector, program type, and status
  • Charts and summary views for leadership teams
  • GCC-focused with growing global coverage
  • Status tracking: Open, Reviewing, Closing Soon, Planned
  • Exportable summaries (coming in V1)
