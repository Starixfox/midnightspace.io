PRODUCT 01

Saudi Opportunity Hub

The core Midnight Space platform. A live, manually curated and filterable dashboard that consolidates over 1,000 investment opportunities, government funding calls, accelerators, grants, and programs across 15 sectors of the Saudi economy.

Designed for teams who need a comprehensive view of what programs are open, closing, or planned — across the full Vision 2030 economic agenda — without spending days pulling information from fragmented government sources.

  • 1,034 investment opportunities tracked and categorised
  • Covers 15 sectors: energy, technology, tourism, healthcare, space, and more
  • Filters by sector, program type, funding source, and status
  • Charts and summary views for leadership teams
  • Status tracking: Open, Reviewing, Closing Soon, Planned
  • Weekly data updates (pilot tier)
  • White-label version and API access (V1)

White-Label Dashboard

A branded version of the Saudi Opportunity Hub, configured for your organisation's sectors and priorities.

API Connection

Programmatic access to the opportunity database so you can feed data directly into your own internal tools and workflows.

Weekly Data Updates

The dataset is refreshed weekly to reflect new programs, changed statuses, and newly identified funding calls.

Weekly Reports

A curated summary delivered weekly, highlighting what is new, what is closing, and what to watch across your sectors of interest.
