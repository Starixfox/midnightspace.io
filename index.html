CORE CAPABILITIES

Built around data, not a single vertical

Everything Midnight Space does is about making Saudi Arabia's investment and opportunity landscape readable, navigable, and actionable.

Opportunity Mapping

Funding calls, grants, accelerators, and government programs across 15 sectors — consolidated in one searchable database with filters by sector, type, and status.

Program Tracking

Monitor open and closed program cycles, recurring calls, and application windows across the full Saudi investment landscape — so you never miss a deadline.

Ecosystem Dashboards

Visual summaries and charts for leadership teams. Turn fragmented government data into a clear picture of where opportunity is concentrating.

Signals & Alerts Planned

Notifications when new funding calls open or programs change status across any tracked sector. Coming in a future version of the platform.
