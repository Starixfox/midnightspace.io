Studio · 01

A small team.
A clear point of view.

Midnight Space is independent by design. We partner with a handful of clients each year, and we do fewer things, more deliberately than the industry expects.

We bring strategy and craft to the same table — positioning that holds up under pressure, interfaces that feel inevitable, and a level of finish that signals what a company actually is.

We're builders first. We ship.

The best digital work happens when fewer people make more deliberate decisions. We organise the studio around that single belief. John Apodaca  ·  Founder, Midnight Space

About · 03

Founded in 2024.
Independent by design.

STUDIO / OBJ-01NIGHT-SIDE EXPOSURE

Midnight Space was founded by John Apodaca in 2024 on a quiet conviction: that the best digital work happens when fewer people make more deliberate decisions.

We're a small studio with a global outlook. We work with early-stage founders building something specific, operators inside larger companies who still care about craft, and institutions that want to look and behave like the future they're investing in.

We're based nowhere in particular, reachable everywhere. We keep the room small, the schedule honest, and the output sharper than the brief.
