WHO USES IT

Use Cases

Different organisations face different data problems when navigating Saudi Arabia's investment landscape. Here is how the Saudi Opportunity Hub addresses them.

USE CASE 01

Investment Promotion Offices

Bodies like Invest Saudi need a comprehensive, real-time view of active investment programs across all sectors to attract foreign capital, brief decision-makers, and identify where the next opportunity concentrations are forming. Manually tracking this across 15 sectors is impractical at scale.

  • Single view of all active funding programs and incentives across 15 sectors
  • Identify which sectors are receiving the most government investment right now
  • Generate briefing summaries for leadership and investor meetings quickly
  • Track Vision 2030 program activity in real time
Discuss your use case
USE CASE 02

Governments & Public Agencies

Ministries and national agencies need a clear picture of what programs exist across the economy, where duplication occurs, and how government investment compares to national targets. The Saudi Opportunity Hub aggregates this from dozens of sources into one dashboard.

  • Track all active and upcoming national programs across 15 sectors
  • Identify funding gaps and overlaps within current government portfolios
  • Benchmark program activity against Vision 2030 strategic targets
  • Build cross-sector briefing summaries for policy teams
Discuss your use case
USE CASE 03

Investors & Funds

Government grant and program activity is one of the most reliable leading indicators of where private capital will flow next. Tracking which sectors receive consistent Saudi public funding across 15 verticals helps investors get ahead of the curve before commercial dealflow follows.

  • Monitor where government money is concentrating across all Saudi sectors
  • Identify verticals with sustained public funding before commercial deals follow
  • Track recurring programs that signal long-term government commitment
  • Use cross-sector ecosystem data to validate or challenge investment theses
Discuss your use case
USE CASE 04

Startups & Ecosystem Builders

Accelerators, clusters, and founders working in Saudi Arabia need to know what funding their companies can apply for — across any sector — and when. Manually tracking government programs across 15 sectors is time-consuming and error-prone without a consolidated platform.

  • Discover all open funding calls relevant to your sector or portfolio
  • Track application windows and avoid missed deadlines across 15 sectors
  • Build a clear view of programs available in your specific verticals
  • Share filtered program views with founders and companies in your network
Discuss your use case
USE CASE 05

Corporate & Foreign Investors

International companies and foreign investors entering Saudi Arabia need a fast, reliable picture of what government programs exist, which sectors are prioritised, and where public incentives align with their business interests — before committing to market entry decisions.

  • Quickly map the full Saudi investment incentive landscape across 15 sectors
  • Identify where public and private investment interests align
  • Understand which programs are open to foreign participation
  • Reduce the research cost of Saudi market entry by weeks
