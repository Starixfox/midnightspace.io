WHO USES IT

Use Cases

Different organisations face different data problems in the space sector. Here is how Midnight Space tools address them.

USE CASE 01

Governments & Agencies

National space agencies and government ministries need a clear picture of what programs exist, where duplication occurs, and how their investments compare to regional peers. That requires data aggregated from dozens of sources — which is exactly what Midnight Space does.

  • Track all active and upcoming national space programs in one place
  • Identify gaps and overlaps in current funding portfolios
  • Benchmark program activity against regional and global peers
  • Build briefing summaries for leadership teams quickly
Discuss your use case
USE CASE 02

Economic Development Offices

Development offices and investment promotion bodies need to understand where public money is going in the space sector before they can design effective incentive structures or identify attraction targets.

  • Map public funding flows across GCC and MENA space programs
  • Identify which sectors and geographies are receiving the most investment
  • Spot programs that signal government commitment to specific subsectors
  • Support economic strategy with data, not assumptions
Discuss your use case
USE CASE 03

Investors & Funds

Government grant activity is one of the most reliable leading indicators of where private capital will flow next in deep tech. Tracking which sectors receive consistent public funding helps investors get ahead of the curve.

  • Monitor where government money is concentrating in space
  • Identify subsectors with sustained public funding — before commercial deals follow
  • Track recurring grant programs that signal long-term government commitment
  • Use ecosystem data to validate or challenge investment theses
Discuss your use case
USE CASE 04

Ecosystem Builders

Accelerators, clusters, and incubators working in the space sector need to know what funding their cohort companies can apply for — and when. Manually tracking government programs across multiple countries is time-consuming and error-prone.

  • Discover open funding calls relevant to your portfolio or cohort
  • Track application windows and avoid missed deadlines
  • Build a clear picture of the programs available in your region
  • Share filtered program views with founders in your network
