Use case 01

Public agencies tracking calls and programs

Government departments and public agencies responsible for space policy or industrial strategy need to know which programs are active, which are closing, and what the funding landscape looks like nationally and internationally.

A filterable, always-updated dashboard replaces hours of manual web research. Leadership can see a summary in minutes. Staff can drill into specifics without starting from scratch each time a new brief is needed.

✓

All relevant programs visible in one view — filtered by region, sector, and status

✓

Charts summarising open calls, funding volumes, and program categories at a glance

✓

Data that can be referenced in briefings and policy documents without manual curation

Use case 02

Economic development teams

Teams tasked with growing a regional or national space economy need to understand where public funding is flowing, where the gaps are, and where the overlaps between programs create inefficiencies.

Midnight Space data helps these teams build evidence-based strategy — identifying program categories that are underserved, tracking how peer countries structure their incentives, and seeing where international programs could complement domestic ones.

✓

Map the gap — see which sectors or regions lack visible funding programs

✓

Compare peer nations' program structures and funding priorities

✓

Identify overlapping incentives that could be rationalised or strengthened

Use case 03

Investors and funds

Government program investment tends to precede commercial venture activity. Investors and funds focused on deep tech or space benefit from understanding where public money is concentrating — it signals where commercial deal flow is likely to emerge.

Midnight Space makes it possible to scan program data quickly — by sector, region, and funding level — without relying on fragmented news sources or manual research across multiple government websites.

✓

See where government investment is concentrating ahead of commercial activity

✓

Track which programs have active accelerators that could surface portfolio companies

✓

Build market context for due diligence and LP reporting without weeks of desk research

Use case 04

Universities and research labs

Academic institutions and research groups need to discover open scholarship calls, fellowships, research grants, and collaborative program opportunities — across multiple funding bodies simultaneously.

Midnight Space aggregates these sources so research offices and faculty can monitor the funding landscape without subscribing to dozens of newsletters or assigning staff to continuous monitoring tasks.

✓

Discover scholarships, fellowships, and research grants across agencies in one place

✓

Track application deadlines and recurring cycles across multiple funding bodies

✓

Identify international collaboration opportunities and co-funding program requirements
