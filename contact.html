WHY REACH OUT

Talk to the founder

Midnight Space is an early-stage project built by one person. There are no sales teams or account managers — when you reach out, you are talking directly to the founder.

We are specifically looking for pilot partners — organisations that have a real need for space ecosystem data and are willing to test an early prototype in exchange for direct input on the product.

What to expect

  • A response from the founder within 48 hours
  • A short call to understand your specific data needs
  • Access to the current prototype for review
  • An honest conversation about what the product can and cannot do today
  • Input on what gets built next if you become a pilot partner

Who we are looking for:
Governments and public agencies · Economic development offices · Space investment funds · Accelerators and ecosystem builders · Anyone navigating the space funding landscape
