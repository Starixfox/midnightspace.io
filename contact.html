Ideal pilot partners

Who we most want to hear from

We can work with almost anyone in the space ecosystem, but these are the organisations where we think the match is strongest right now.

Government space agencies

National or regional agencies that track space programs and funding — especially in the GCC and wider MENA region. We are most relevant to teams doing policy, strategy, or economic development.

Investment offices with space focus

Funds, family offices, or sovereign wealth vehicles that are building or refining a space investment thesis and need better program and funding intelligence as part of their research process.

Ecosystem builders — accelerators & clusters

Organisations building a space startup ecosystem who need to know what programs, grants, and accelerators are available to their community — in one place, kept up to date.
