MILESTONES

Four stages of development

Prototype Stage

  • Live demo of Saudi Space Opportunity Hub
  • Manually curated program database
  • Basic filters by region, sector, and status
  • Charts and summary views
  • Solo founder — direct pilot partner access

HOW WE BUILD

Honest about what we are

Solo founded

One person building, iterating, and talking to users. That means fast decisions and direct conversations — no layers.

Prototype first

We ship working demos before polished products. Feedback from real users shapes every version of the roadmap.

Partner-shaped

Pilot partners have direct input on what gets built next. If you have a specific need, we want to hear about it.

No hype

We are not a launch company. We are building data tools. The roadmap reflects what is actually achievable, not what sounds impressive.

SHAPE THE PRODUCT

Want to shape the product?
